We are RTU and LU BITL students from RBS, this questionnaire consist of test and survey. Questionnaire will be used for gathering data for a psychology research project. The aim of the research project is to determine the correlation between stress and short-term memory. The results will not be public and will be presented to a narrow, selected audience. Test consist of a number of short-term memory exercises about memorizing a span of digits. The survey consist of two demographic questions and ten Likert-type questions where respondent is required to evaluate the frequency of experiencing a particular emotions. The approximate time to complete the test and survey is 6 minutes. Test and survey have detailed instructions for execution.
